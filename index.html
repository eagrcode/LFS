Who We Are

London Furniture Studio are a Luxury Bespoke Fitted and Freestanding Furniture manufacturer specialising in designing and handcrafting all varieties of furniture in the High End residential and commercial market. With the combined experience of over a decade in Fine Furniture, we can proudly offer a truly beautiful and long lasting product.


We offer all forms of furniture and cabinetry including Walk-in Closets, Fitted Wardrobes, Dressing Rooms, Bespoke Kitchens, Media Rooms, Living Rooms, Bathrooms and Unique Freestanding Pieces. Our range of materials stems from man made boards like veneered MDF and Melamine all the way to Fine Timber, Brass and Mirror/Glass.
